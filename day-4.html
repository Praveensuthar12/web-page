Fruits are a crucial part of our diet. They are loaded with essential vitamins and minerals and, more importantly, all the sweetness and enjoyable natural flavours! Although there are hundreds of different varieties of fruits worldwide, mangoes, apples, strawberries, and oranges are the top spots for favourites among children. Not surprisingly, this is an essay topic that students are often asked to write

Mango is my favourite fruit. It is sweet and juicy. Mangoes come in different colours, like yellow, orange, and red. Mangoes grow on tall trees with big green leaves. The skin of a mango is smooth, and the flesh is soft. Mangoes have an ample seed in the middle called a stone.
